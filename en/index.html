Welcome

Welcome to EAS logistics, your partner for Express Logistics worldwide. EAS logistic is a dynamic and flexible logistics company, with the most experience in transport by air, sea, and road around the world. We transport various goods such as all kinds of spare parts, all types of machines, project materials, personal effects, cars and containers.

We have been doing this business very successfully for the past seven years. In addition, we have practical and specialized knowledge in transporting goods from Europe to developing countries in general.

Our company has been working in close cooperation with many recognised big airline companies such as Air France, Lufthansa, Ethiopian Airways and Emirates to deliver your goods on time.

We offer services in the following areas:

  • We can look for and/or purchase any kind of machine, spare parts or other products you want from Europe and transport them to your destination with a very fair transportation charges,
  • We provide special advices and answer your individual questions on export and transportation issues. For those from East and Central Africa, consultations can also be done in their national languages,
  • We facilitate the provision of transport insurance to avoid risks on transporting your possessions or goods,
  • We offer special services to Diplomat and people returning back to their home countries,
  • We provide pick up services of your goods anywhere in Europe
  • We make export packing for your transport possessions
  • We offer transport services to organisations/companies participating in Trade Fair, Export evidence and customs clearance
